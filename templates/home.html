COMP90054 Alternative Pacman Competition


I've made a alternative online competition for the Unimelb COMP90054 Pacman Contest, since testing once a day is really not enough when you're constantly making changes and want to know how it stands against other teams' agents.

Please don't abuse this. I slapped this together in a couple of hours and it will probably break even if it's just left to sit idle for the next 24 hours.

(Bit late I know but, hey, it's something)


Use this form to submit your own agent to compete against other teams. Please submit a .py file renamed to your team name. You can reupload your agent multiple times, as long as it has the same team name it will overwrite your previously uploaded version.


Use this form to request a match between two uploaded agents. The request will be put into a queue, and once the match is played out the logs and replays can be downloaded from the Results page.
